Educational Support & Applications Req 9

Economics (Exponential)

Exponential models describe data that grows at a constant percentage rate. Essential for:

  • GDP Growth: Tracking national production over decades.
  • Calculating compound interest and inflation rates.
  • Modeling initial population growth in biological ecosystems.

Engineering (Polynomial)

Polynomial curves are excellent for mapping complex, non-linear physical phenomena:

  • Material Stress: Mapping stress-strain curves before fracture.
  • Aerodynamics: Modeling wind resistance at various velocities.
  • Sensor Calibration for non-linear hardware noise.
